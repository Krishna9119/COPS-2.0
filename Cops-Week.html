COPS WEEK

The Club of Programmers(COPS), IIT BHU is back again with a bang. We are going to organize a COPS Week, especially for Freshers from February 5 to February 12, where we will hold events for Competitive Programming, Machine Learning, Development, and Infosec. Prizes worth 1000 INR to top three performers in each event and get a chance to get into COPS.

For regular updates on COPS Week join our official Google Group. Also, for discussion, announcements, resources for all the COPS events freshers can join our discord server. You can get an invite to the server through this portal.

Description about different events are as follows:

  • NP Compete

    Competitive Programming Group presents NP Compete a 48-hr CP contest. You can register for Np Compete 2k21 here.

  • Ctrl Shift Intelligence

    ML Group presents Ctrl Shift Intelligence, a short quiz followed by a 3 day computer vision competition. For quiz register here. For competition register here.

  • WannaHack CTF

    InfoSec Group present WannaHack a 24hr capture the flag event. You can register for WannaHack CTF here.

  • DebugIt

    Dev Group presents DebugIt, a week long dev sprint. You can register for DebugIt here.
